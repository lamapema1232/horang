Infrastructure


Our college is committed to providing a conducive environment for learning, research, and personal growth. Here are some of the key infrastructure components we have:

Academic Buildings:
We boast state-of-the-art academic buildings equipped with digital classrooms, lecture halls, and seminar rooms. These spaces are designed to facilitate interactive and engaging learning experiences.

Administrative Facilities:
We have dedicated administrative buildings that house various departments, including admissions, finance, academic affairs, and student services. These facilities ensure efficient management and smooth functioning of college operations.

Spacious Recreational Areas:
At Xavier International College, students get an opportunity to engage themselves in Indoor and Outdoor sports activities (basketball, Table Tennis, Chess, badminton) to promote physical fitness and mental well-being.

Campus Security and Safety:
The safety and security of our campus community are of utmost importance to us. We have implemented comprehensive security measures, including surveillance cameras, access control systems, and emergency response protocols, to ensure a secure and peaceful environment for everyone.

Medical Facilities:
The college provides a health service to ensure the well-being of the students and staff. This includes a provision for college nurses. We also provide first aid services and medicines for general medicines with the consent of the parents.




Library

Xavier International College has a well-equipped library with a wide range of books, journals, and reference materials that are relevant to the A-levels curriculum. Xavier International offers a wide collection of materials, books, and other media that are accessible to students in physical and digital forms. In Xavier International, the library occupies a prominent position and it is an important and integral part of the teaching program. We have materials arranged in a specified order according to a library classification system so that items can be located quickly and collections may be browsed efficiently. Our academic library serves the students and faculty to provide support in research, consultancy, and resource linkage for students and faculty. Our library offers workshops and courses outside of formal, graded coursework, which are meant to provide students with the tools necessary to succeed in their programs. Our workshops provide students with skills that can help them achieve success in their academic careers (and often, in their future occupations), which they may not learn inside the classroom. The academic library provides a quiet study space for students on campus; it also provides group study space. Our main objective and function of the library are to provide resources necessary for research in the field of interest, to help the teacher in keeping abreast of development in their field, to open the door to the world of books that lie beyond the borders of one's own field of specialization for the success of all formal programs at Xavier International. The library also provides access to online resources and e-books.



Labs


Science Lab

Xavier International College has modern science labs that are equipped with the latest equipment and technology for students to conduct experiments and practicals as per the A-levels curriculum. The labs are designed to provide a safe and conducive learning environment.

Computer Lab

Xavier International College has computer labs with high-speed internet connectivity, printers, and other essential software that are required for the A-levels curriculum. These labs are open to students for their academic and research purposes.


Technology

Technology-based teaching is becoming increasingly popular as it allows students to learn and study in a more interactive and engaging manner. Here are some ways that technology is being used in coaching institutes to enhance the learning experience: 1. Digital learning solution: We use a comprehensive digital learning solution that includes videos, animations, and interactive simulations. This technology-based learning solution enables students to learn at their own pace and provides a personalized learning experience. 2. Concept-Based Videos: We use concept-based videos to teach students complex concepts in a simplified manner. These videos are designed to explain concepts and theories visually, making it easier for students to understand and retain information. 3. Interactive Whiteboards: Interactive whiteboards are also used in. These whiteboards allow teachers to present complex information visually and engage students in interactive learning activities. Students can also collaborate and participate in group discussions, making the learning experience more engaging and interactive. 4. Online Learning Platforms: It offers online learning platforms that provide students with access to study materials, practice tests, and video lectures. These platforms allow students to learn at their own pace and provide a flexible learning experience that fits their individual needs. 5. Online Test Series: We conduct online test series for competitive exams. These online test series simulate the actual exam environment, allowing students to practice and gain confidence before the actual exam.




Health and wellness services

Xavier International College has a health and well-being center, which provides students with medical services, counseling, and wellness programs. The center also organizes health and wellness workshops, seminars, and events to promote healthy habits and well-being among students.


Transportation

Xavier International College provides transportation facilities for students who live far away from the college. The college has a fleet of buses that transport students to and from the college.

Accommodation


Xavier International College takes great pride in providing comprehensive and well-rounded hostel facilities to its students. The hostel rooms are spacious and well-maintained, offering comfortable living space for students. Each room is furnished with modern furniture, including study desks, chairs, beds, wardrobes, and bookshelves, ensuring that students have everything they need to study and relax. The hostel mess provides nutritious and delicious meals to students. The menu is diverse, catering to different dietary preferences and requirements. The college ensures that the food is prepared under strict hygiene standards, prioritizing the students' health and well-being. The hostel provides laundry services that are managed by a trusted vendor who handles the laundry services on behalf of the hostel. Students can easily drop off their laundry at designated locations within the hostel on designated days, and the vendor takes care of washing and drying the clothes. This service relieves students from the burden of doing laundry themselves, allowing them to prioritize their studies and engage in other activities without worrying about their laundry needs. The hostel provides well-maintained sports facilities, including a sports ground, basketball court, and indoor games room. These facilities encourage students to engage in physical activities, promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. They also foster a sense of camaraderie and teamwork among the residents. The college provides transportation facilities for students between the hostel and the college campus. Buses and vans operate on designated timings, ensuring that students can conveniently commute to and from their classes without any transportation-related concerns. The hostel includes common areas and recreational spaces where students can relax and socialize. These areas are designed to provide a comfortable and friendly atmosphere, allowing students to engage in activities such as reading, group discussions, or simply enjoying conversations with their peers. A dedicated hostel warden is appointed to oversee the well-being and safety of the hostel residents. The warden ensures discipline, monitors the hostel premises, and addresses any concerns or issues that students may have. Their presence provides a sense of security and support to the students, creating a conducive living environment. The hostel offers additional classes conducted by experienced faculty members or qualified tutors. These classes provide academic support and guidance to students, focusing on specific subjects or helping them with challenging topics. The availability of extra classes within the hostel premises allows students to seek academic assistance in a familiar and convenient environment.
